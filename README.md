###  3D Face Reconstruction From in the Wild Images — final year dissertation

Using the DECA pipeline I investigated the possiblity of integrating a modified version of SkinNet (X. Li, G. C. Guarnera, A. Lin, and A. Ghosh, “Realistic facial age transformation with 3D uplifting,”) to estimate and edit chromophores to realistically render realistic face and skin. The project centres around improving the accuracy of skin tone in ambiguously lit images by estimating chromophores and reconstructing the skin from a biophysical baseline. Ensuring lighting that polutes the image would never result in an unobtainable skin tone. 
This project was bottlenecked by the default DECA delighting stage, various attempts were made to accomodate for specural reflections and shadows however the ambigous reality of ground truth images lended itself to more complex and advanced approaches, such as those demostrated by S3Face. 
Although the approach taken in this project had fundamental flaws, I did manage to increase the accuracy of DECAs skin tone on re lit images. The next steps to take with this research are into more advanced (but lighter weight that the full Stable Diffusion Neural Approach) de lighting stages. Then adapting the DECA rendering stage to include Sub Surface Scattering to properly utilise the estimated chromophores.

CS2 Advanced Positioning Metric - FPS positioning metrics have long been poluted by lucky angles and dynamic gameplay that makes it inherently hard to quanitfy. This novel approach leveraging large CS2 demo datasets aims to establish what defines a play as *Lucky* and what defines one as **skill**. This is established by combining all other metrics that are already well documented into a multi layered model. Current outputs suggest good correlation, pro players are getting rated consistently better than average, something that other industry leaders have struggled to confidently match without using other statistics as bias. This model uses other statistics to eliminate bad plays to encourage position as opposed to other stats boosting position rating.
